Cake Decorator

Involves crafting and decorating cakes, pastries, and other baked goods for various occasions, often with a focus on customer requests and event themes. They prepare cakes, apply icing and decorations, and may also manage inventory, clean work areas, and provide customer service.

Line Cook

Prepares and cooks food at a designated station in a kitchen, working closely with the sous chef and head chef to ensure efficient service and consistent quality. Their responsibilities include preparing ingredients, cooking menu items, and plating dishes according to recipes and standards.

Baker/Baker Assistant

prepares and bakes a variety of items like bread, pastries, cakes, and pies, often operating in bakeries or restaurants. They are responsible for mixing ingredients, preparing dough, filling and decorating baked goods, and ensuring timely completion of orders. Bakers also perform quality checks on ingredients and finished products, and may be involved in creating new recipes or modifying existing ones.
