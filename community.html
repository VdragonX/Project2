Donation

Our commitment to serve our communities is one of our greatest sources of pride and core to our founder's legacy. Rosa Porto had a passion for feeding people, and just like most grandmas, was heartbroken to see anyone go hungry. In pursuit of continuing her personal mission, we bake all our breads and pastries fresh daily and donate them nightly.

Religious

For those observing Lent, Porto's Bakery offers limited-time seafood-themed options, particularly their popular potato balls, which are available with a seafood ragout. Other options include regular pastries and desserts that are suitable for a Lenten diet. While Porto's has many options, their Lent-friendly offerings may include items without dairy or meat, such as certain fruit-filled pastries or cakes.
